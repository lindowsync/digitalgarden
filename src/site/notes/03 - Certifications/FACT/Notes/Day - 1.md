# 📖 TOPIC 1: FOUNDATIONS OF FORENSIC SCIENCE

---

## 🔵 1. YE HOTA KYA HAI?

```
Forensic Science = Science + Law ka combination

"Forensic" word Latin ke "Forum" se aaya hai
Forum = Public court/debate place in ancient Rome

Simple words mein:
Scientific methods use karke crime solve karna
= Forensic Science
```

**Real Example:**

> Murder scene pe ek hair mila. Usse DNA lab mein test kiya. DNA match hua suspect se. Court mein present kiya. Criminal pakda gaya. **Yahi hai Forensic Science.**

# 📖 TOPIC 2: TYPES OF EVIDENCE

---

## 🔵 Evidence Kya Hota Hai?

```
Evidence = Wo cheez jo court mein 
crime prove karne mein help kare
```

## 🔵 EVIDENCE KE TYPES — CHART

```
EVIDENCE
├── Physical Evidence (Tangible/Murthi)
│   ├── Weapons, clothes, documents
│   ├── Blood, hair, fingerprints
│   └── Bullets, drugs, soil
│
├── Testimonial Evidence (Zubaani)
│   ├── Eyewitness statement
│   ├── Expert opinion
│   └── Victim's statement
│
├── Documentary Evidence (Kagzi)
│   ├── Written documents
│   ├── Photographs, CCTV footage
│   └── Digital records
│
└── Circumstantial Evidence (Parikaaramic)
    ├── Indirect evidence
    ├── Suggests guilt but doesn't prove directly
    └── Example: Accused ke paas murder weapon tha
```

## 🔵 OBJECTIVE vs SUBJECTIVE EVIDENCE

|Type|Example|Exam Trick|
|---|---|---|
|**Objective**|Fingerprint, DNA, Iris|Scientific — machine se verify hota|
|**Subjective**|Eyewitness statement|Human dependent — bias ho sakta hai|

**PYQ 2018:** _"Which evidence is subjective in nature?"_ **Answer:** Eye Witness Statement ✅

---

## 🔵 CLASS vs INDIVIDUAL CHARACTERISTICS

```
CLASS Characteristics:
= Ek group ko identify karta hai
Example: Ye blood group 'O' hai
         Ye Nike ka shoe hai

INDIVIDUAL Characteristics:
= Ek specific person/object identify karta hai
Example: Ye fingerprint sirf RAM ka hai
         Ye bullet sirf IS GUN se fire hui
```

**Memory Trick:**

> 🏫 **Class** = School ki class (sab ek jaisi) 👤 **Individual** = Sirf ek banda unique

# 📖 TOPIC 3: CHAIN OF CUSTODY

---

## 🔵 Chain of Custody Kya Hai?

```
Chain of Custody = Evidence ka travel log

Crime Scene se Court tak
Evidence kiske haath mein gaya
Kab gaya, Kaise gaya, Kyun gaya
Sab document karna = Chain of Custody
```

**Real Life Example:**

```
Murder scene → Blood sample mila
↓
IO (Investigating Officer) ne collect kiya [Sign + Time]
↓
Sealed karke FSL bheja [Sign + Time]
↓
FSL scientist ne receive kiya [Sign + Time]
↓
Analysis ki [Sign + Time]
↓
Court mein present kiya [Sign + Time]

Ye poora record = CHAIN OF CUSTODY
```

## 🔵 CHAIN OF CUSTODY KYUN IMPORTANT HAI?

```
Agar chain toot jaaye:
❌ Evidence tampered mana jaayega
❌ Court mein admissible nahi hoga
❌ Criminal free ho sakta hai

Agar chain intact ho:
✅ Evidence authentic hai
✅ Court mein admissible hoga
✅ Criminal ko saza milegi
```

## 🔵 INTEGRITY vs AUTHENTICITY

|Term|Matlab|Example|
|---|---|---|
|**Integrity**|Evidence modify nahi hua|Blood sample contaminate nahi hua|
|**Authenticity**|Evidence genuine hai|Ye wahi evidence hai jo scene se mila|
|**Chain of Custody**|Dono prove karta hai|Document trail se|

**PYQ 2018:** _"Integrity of clue material is proved if?"_ **Answer:** Chain of custody is maintained ✅

**PYQ 2022:** _"Proof of proper chain of custody allows?"_ **Answer:** Evidence to be admitted to court ✅

**PYQ 2024:** _"Integrity of Chain of Custody refers to?"_ **Answer:** Keeping evidence in pristine condition ✅

## 🔵 MOST IMPORTANT SECTIONS — TABLE

|Section|Act|Subject|Memory Trick|
|---|---|---|---|
|**Section 45**|Indian Evidence Act 1872|**Expert Opinion**|45 = **4+5=9** = **N**ine = **N**ishaan (expert ka)|
|**Section 47**|Indian Evidence Act 1872|Opinion on Handwriting|47 = Hand**writing**|
|**Section 73**|Indian Evidence Act 1872|Comparison of signatures/handwriting by court|73 = Court compare karta|
|**Section 293**|CrPC 1973|Expert evidence admissible|293 = **2+9+3=14** = Expert **admit**|
|**Section 174**|CrPC|Police Inquest|174 = **1** body, **7**4 questions|
|**Section 304**|CrPC|Free legal aid|304 = **Free** (F=6, ignore)|
|**Section 79A**|IT Act 2000|Examiner of Electronic Evidence|79A = **7+9=16** = Digital **16** bit|
|**Section 66A**|IT Act 2000|**Struck down** by Supreme Court|66A = **Gone** (SC ne hataya)|

---

## 🔵 NABL Kya Hai?

```
NABL = National Accreditation Board 
       for Testing and Calibration Laboratories

Headquarter: GURUGRAM
Accreditation Standard: ISO/IEC 17025
Full form of CAB: Conformity Assessment Body
Renewal: 6 months pehle apply karna hota hai
```

## 🔵 ISO/IEC 17025 Kya Hai?

```
= Forensic labs ke liye quality standard

Iska matlab:
✅ Lab ke results reliable hain
✅ Proper procedures follow ho rahi hain
✅ Equipment calibrated hai
✅ Staff qualified hai

Primary purpose of accrediting forensic labs:
→ QUALITY ASSURANCE (financial gain nahi)
```
